A simple quadratic voting machine with OECD Better Life Index categories

You get 100 points, but the more you vote for one thing the less that vote is worth (the number gets squared).

Drag the sliders to indicate your preferences. If you can't see them all you can zoom out a bit. If you would like to make this better please be my guest!
